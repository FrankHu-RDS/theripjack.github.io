The Ripjack™ safely strips URD cable with the push of a button,

Preventing injuries and saving valuable time and money

The patented Ripjack is the first power tool to remove the outer jacket from stranded concentric underground primary cable, replacing current manual methods. The Ripjack solves currently significant issues with safety efficiency, and user friendliness in the cable preparation process. The Ripjack works on all sizes of stranded concentric URD cable from #2 to 1500 MCM, with no adjustments needed between different sizes of cable.
